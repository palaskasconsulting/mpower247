About M-Power Fitness

M-Power Fitness is Roanoke, Virginia's premier 24-hour gym. We offer state-of-the-art equipment, expert personal training, and a variety of group fitness classes including yoga, HIIT, and strength training. Whether you're aiming to lose weight, build muscle, or enhance overall wellness, our community-focused gym is here to support your journey.


Located in the heart of Roanoke, we provide flexible memberships, tailored fitness plans, and nutrition coaching to help you achieve your goals. Join us today and discover the best fitness experience in the area!
